ABOUT METEORA

Meteora, the flagship event of Don Bosco College, Panjim, is an eagerly anticipated celebration that marks the highlight of our academic calendar. Held annually, Meteora is a two-day extravaganza filled with a diverse array of activities and competitions, captivating the hearts and minds of students and participants alike.
This year, Meteora is set to unfold its magic on the 1st and 2nd of February, transforming the auditorium of Don Bosco College into a vibrant hub of creativity and excitement.

Celebrated for its myriad of events and vibrant atmosphere, Meteora is not just an event; it's an experience. From thrilling competitions to captivating performances, Meteora offers something for everyone. As participants engage in friendly rivalry and showcase their skills, the event becomes a melting pot of creativity, innovation, and camaraderie.
Beyond the competitions, Meteora fosters a sense of community and belonging, providing an opportunity for students to forge lasting friendships and memories that will be cherished for years to come. Adding to the allure of Meteora is its annual theme, which injects fresh energy and excitement into the festivities. Each year, a new theme sets the tone for the event, inspiring participants to explore new ideas and perspectives. This dynamic approach not only adds to the vibrancy of the event but also ensures that every edition of Meteora is unique and memorable, offering participants a fresh and exciting experience every time.
